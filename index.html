    Standard Curriculum

    Kinder (3-6) Years

    Children aged 3–6 years, often called "kinders," are in a critical stage of growth and development. This period is vital for nurturing curiosity and emotional resilience.

    Elementary School

    Elementary school is the foundation of education, typically for children aged 5 to 12. It focuses on basic subjects like reading, writing, math, and social skills to build a strong learning base.

    Middle (10-16) Years

    The middle years , from 10 to 16, are a crucial stage of development marked by physical changes during puberty, emotional growth, and identity exploration. It's a time when individuals become more independent and develop critical thinking skills.
